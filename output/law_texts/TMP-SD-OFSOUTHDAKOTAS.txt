5             BE IT ENACTED BY THE LEGISLATURE OF THE STATE OF TEXAS:

6             SECTIONA1.AASubchapter B, Chapter 43, Penal Code, is amended

7    by adding Section 43.235 to read as follows:

8             Sec.A43.235.AAPOSSESSION,           PROMOTION,      OR    PRODUCTION     OF

9    CERTAIN VISUAL MATERIAL APPEARING TO DEPICT CHILD.                    (a)   In this

10   section:

11                 (1)AA"Promote"      has    the    meaning     assigned   by   Section

12   43.25.

13                 (2)AA"Visual     material"        has   the   meaning    assigned   by

14   Section 43.26.

15            (b)AAA person commits an offense if the person:

16                 (1)AAknowingly possesses, accesses with intent to view,

17   or promotes obscene visual material containing a depiction that

18   appears to be of a child younger than 18 years of age engaging in

19   activities    described   by     Section     43.21(a)(1)(B),        regardless    of

20   whether the depiction is an image of an actual child, a cartoon or

21   animation, or an image created using an artificial intelligence

22   application or other computer software; or

23                 (2)AAuses an image of an actual child younger than 18

24   years of age at the time the image was made with the intent to train

1    an    artificial   intelligence   model   to   produce   visual   material

2    constituting child pornography under Section 43.26.

3            (c)AAAn offense under this section is a state jail felony,

4    except that the offense is:

5                  (1)AAa felony of the third degree if it is shown on the

6    trial of the offense that the person has been previously convicted

7    one time of an offense under this section or Section 43.23, 43.26,

8    43.261, or 43.262; or

9                  (2)AAa felony of the second degree if it is shown on the

10   trial of the offense that the person has been previously convicted

11   two or more times of an offense under this section, Section 43.23,

12   43.26, 43.261, or 43.262, or any combination of those offenses.

13           (d)AAIf conduct constituting an offense under this section

14   also constitutes an offense under another law, the actor may be

15   prosecuted under this section, the other law, or both.
