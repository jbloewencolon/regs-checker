However, Resolutions and Concurrent Resolutions are considered identical if the only difference is the word "House" or "Senate." Similar bill Companion bills that are substantially similar in text or have substantial portions of text that are largely the same. Compare bill Bills that have selected provisions that are similar in text. Linked bill A bill that is contingent upon passage of another bill within the same chamber, e.g., a trust fund bill, a bill providing a public record exemption, or an implementing bill.
