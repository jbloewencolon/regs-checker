    Be it enacted by the General Assembly of Virginia:
1. That the Code of Virginia is amended by adding in Title 19.2 a chapter numbered 1.3, consisting of a
section numbered 19.2-11.14, as follows:
                                                 CHAPTER 1.3.
                                ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE-BASED TOOLS.
    § 19.2-11.14. Use of artificial intelligence-based tool.
    A. As used in this section, "artificial intelligence-based tool" means any machine-based system or
algorithm, including machine learning models, predictive analytics, and decision support systems, that
analyze data and generate recommendations or predictions.
    B. All decisions related to the pre-trial detention or release, prosecution, adjudication, sentencing,
probation, parole, correctional supervision, or rehabilitation of criminal offenders shall be made by the
judicial officer or other person charged with making such decision. No such decision shall be made without
the involvement of a human decision-maker. The use of any recommendation or prediction from an artificial
intelligence-based tool shall be subject to any challenge or objection permitted by law.
