 3    LONG TITLE
 4    General Description:
 5        This bill concerns the use of artificial intelligence by law enforcement.
 6    Highlighted Provisions:
 7        This bill:
 8        ▸ defines terms;
 9        ▸ requires a law enforcement agency to have a policy concerning the law enforcement
10    agency's use of generative artificial intelligence;
11        ▸ requires a police report or other law enforcement record to include a disclaimer if the
12    report or record was created wholly or partially by using generative artificial
13    intelligence; and
14        ▸ requires the author of a police report or other law enforcement record that was created
15    wholly or partially by using generative artificial intelligence to certify that the author has
16    read and reviewed the report or record for accuracy.

28                             Part 6. Law Enforcement Agency Procedure Requirements

29              53-25-601 . Definitions.
30        As used in this part:
31     (1) "Artificial intelligence" means a machine-based system that can, for a given set of
32         human-defined objectives, make predictions, recommendations, or decisions influencing
33         real or virtual environments.
34     (2) "Generative artificial intelligence" means artificial intelligence technology that is
35         capable of creating content such as text, audio, image, or video based on patterns learned
36         from large volumes of data rather than being explicitly programmed with rules.
37              Section 2. Section 53-25-602 is enacted to read:
38              53-25-602 . Use of generative artificial intelligence by law enforcement -- Policy --
39     Requirements.
40     (1)(a) A law enforcement agency shall have a policy concerning the use of generative
41         artificial intelligence by employees of the law enforcement agency in the course and
42         scope of the law enforcement agency's work.
43         (b) The policy described in Subsection (1)(a) shall:
44                (i) include the requirements described in Subsection (2); and
45                (ii) provide employees of the law enforcement agency with information concerning
46                    the use of generative artificial intelligence, including:
47                    (A) which generative artificial intelligence technologies the employees of the law
48                        enforcement agency may use;
49                    (B) the uses and tasks for which generative artificial intelligence is permitted;
50                    (C) the importance of reviewing content generated by generative artificial
51                        intelligence; and
52                    (D) an acknowledgment that a violation of the policy described in Subsection
53                        (1)(a) may result in administrative disciplinary action by the head of the law
54                        enforcement agency.
55     (2) A written police report or other law enforcement record that was created wholly or
56         partially by using generative artificial intelligence shall:
57         (a) contain within the report or record a disclaimer that the report or record contains
58                content generated by artificial intelligence; and
59         (b) include a certification by the author of the report or record that the author has read
60                and reviewed the report or record for accuracy.
