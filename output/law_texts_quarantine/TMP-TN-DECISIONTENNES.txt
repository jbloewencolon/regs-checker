6           SECTIONA1.AATitle 4, Civil Practice and Remedies Code, is

7    amended by adding Chapter 100B to read as follows:

8             CHAPTER 100B. LIABILITY FOR FINANCIAL EXPLOITATION

9           Sec.A100B.001.AADEFINITIONS.       In this chapter:

10               (1)AA"Artificial intelligence" means a machine-based

11   system that can, for a given set of explicit or implicit objectives,

12   make   predictions,   recommendations,    or   decisions   that   influence

13   real or virtual environments.

14               (2)AA"Artificially generated media" means an image, an

15   audio file, a video file, a radio broadcast, written text, or other

16   media created or modified using artificial intelligence or other

17   computer software with the intent to deceive.

18               (3)AA"Financial exploitation" has the meaning assigned

19   by Section 281.001, Finance Code.

20               (4)AA"Phishing    communication"       means   an   attempt   to

21   deceive or manipulate a person into providing personal, financial,

22   or     identifying    information       through     e-mail,       electronic

23   communication, or other digital means.

24          Sec.A100B.002.AACAUSE OF ACTION FOR DISSEMINATION OF CERTAIN

3          SECTIONA2.AASubchapter D, Chapter 32, Penal Code, is amended

4    by adding Section 32.56 to read as follows:

5          Sec.A32.56.AAFINANCIAL      ABUSE   USING   ARTIFICIALLY     GENERATED

6    MEDIA OR PHISHING.    (a)   In this section:

7               (1)AA"Artificially      generated      media"   has   the   meaning

8    assigned by Section 100B.001, Civil Practice and Remedies Code.

9               (2)AA"Financial      abuse"    has   the   meaning    assigned   by

10   Section 32.55.

11         (b)AAA person commits an offense if the person knowingly

12   engages in financial abuse:

13              (1)AAthrough the use of artificially generated media

14   disseminated to another person; or

15              (2)AAby deceiving or manipulating another person into

16   providing personal, financial, or identifying information through

17   e-mail, electronic communication, or other digital means.

18         (c)AAAn offense under this section is:

19              (1)AAa Class B misdemeanor if the value of the property

20   taken, appropriated, obtained, retained, or used is less than $100;

21              (2)AAa Class A misdemeanor if the value of the property

22   taken, appropriated, obtained, retained, or used is $100 or more

23   but less than $750;

24              (3)AAa state jail felony if the value of the property

25   taken, appropriated, obtained, retained, or used is $750 or more

26   but less than $2,500;

27              (4)AAa felony of the third degree if the value of the
