Bill Title: Relating to the use of artificial intelligence in campaign communications; declaring an emergency.
Catchline/Summary: Requires a disclosure of the use of synthetic media in campaign communications. Digest: The Act would require a disclosure of the use of AI or other similar technology in campaign ads. The Act would create a way to enforce the requirement and to impose a fine for violations. (Flesch Readability Score: 60.7). Requires a disclosure of the use of synthetic media in campaign communications. Provides for the enforcement of the requirement. Subjects a violation of the requirement to a civil penalty not to exceed $10,000. Exempts certain entities and content from the requirement. Declares an emergency, effective on passage.

Registration Instructions / Language Access Services Information

  • Submit the form below to testify or comment either in person or remotely by video or phone. After completing the required fields (*), click the “Submit Registration to Testify” button.

  • Registration ends 30 minutes prior to the start of the meeting. After that time, the registration system will close.
  • After registering and seeing a confirmation screen, you will receive an email with meeting details. If you do not receive a confirmation email, check your email spam folder.
  • Registration does not guarantee that you will have an opportunity to speak; the chair may determine that time for public testimony will be limited.
